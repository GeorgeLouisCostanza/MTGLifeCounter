MTG Life Counter for Android

"Magic: The Gathering"-Life Counter for Android can be used to keep track of life and poison counters as well as supporting several gamemodes (such as planechase and archenemy).

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

What is this?

This App keeps track of all your resources during a Magic: The Gathering (tm) match, namely life and poison counters. You are able to choose between different game modes such as 1vs1, Two-Headed-Giant or Archenemy and the life and poison counters will adjust automatically.

The App also supports different dice so that you do not have to carry your dice bag around with you everytime. Additionally, the App supports archenemy and planechase gamemodes: you can use the App as a Planchase or Scheme deck.

All in all, this App is a companion for every magic player.

How to install?

Download the App (.apk file) here. Load the MTGLifeCounter.apk onto the SD-Card of your Android phone. Browse to the file and open it. Click install. That's it. Note: Make sure you allow installation of Apps from unknown sources.
